Mission

Our mission is simply to produce the best-sounding ukes on the planet at an affordable price. Quality materials, expert craftsmanship and an innovative tunable bridge make these ukes the only one you'll ever want to play.

Our Ukes

All of our ukes are crafted of solid mahogany, joined by dovetail to a mahogany neck. They feature cedar linings, inlaid rosette, our unique, patented tunable bridge and a bone saddle. Each is available in left- and right-handed versions.

Our Team

Alex

Jessica

Co-Founders

Alex and Jessica have been playing ukulele together for five years with their band, "Need More Uke". They realized that all ukuleles have a common issue - as one moves up the neck, the uke goes out of tune. The only way to fix this problem was to move the bridge, but moving the bridge for one string put the others out of wack. So they came up with an ingenious solution: have a separate, moveable bridge section for each string. They co-founded A Uke Above in 2015 to bring this idea to the ukulele world.

Tom

Tom - Head Luthier

As a master luthier, Tom has studied under the best. Working with Alex and Jessica, he came up with an innovative way to implement the moveable bridge system and scale it so that it worked as well on a soprano uke as on the larger baritone uke.
